Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Coastal Roofing & Siding (“we”, “us” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website https://www.coastalroofingandsidingma.com/ (the “Site”) and when you contact us by phone, email, or form.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information such as your name, phone number, email address, and address when you fill out forms or contact us.
  • Project details related to your home, property, and services you request (roofing, siding, decks, trim, etc.).
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected through analytics tools and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your requests, questions, and estimates.
  • Schedule visits, inspections, and provide roofing and siding services.
  • Improve our Site, services, and customer experience.
  • Send important updates, appointment confirmations, and follow-ups.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analyze traffic. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may affect some features of the Site.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only with:

  • Service providers and partners who help us operate the Site, schedule projects, or deliver our services.
  • Authorities if required to comply with law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy and to comply with legal or business requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Requesting access to the data we hold about you.
  • Requesting corrections if your information is inaccurate.
  • Requesting deletion of your information, when applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Our Site may contain links to other websites (for example, Google reviews or social media). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party sites.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
